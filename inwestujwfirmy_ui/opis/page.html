Startupbidder is a new company out of Dusseldorf, Germany, that provides crowdfunding for startup entrprenuers. Traditionally, crowdfunding sites focus on pre-sales of products, which deny any upside for investors should the company become successful. Furthermore, large number of small investors, often numbering in the thousands, pose regulatory problems for companies seeking to sell shares in early investment rounds...
